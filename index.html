BIOGRAPHY

Hello, and welcome to my homepage. I am a doctoral researcher at Johannes Gutenberg University Mainz, working in astroparticle physics with the IceCube experiment. I completed my high school education at Loyola High School, Jamshedpur, India, graduating in 2016. My undergraduate degree was from the Birla Institute of Technology and Science, Pilani, India, which was completed in 2021. Following this, I pursued a two-year Master’s program in particle & astroparticle physics at Johannes Gutenberg University Mainz, where I defended my Master’s thesis in 2023.

The purpose of this website is to bring together my research activities, publications, academic background, and contact information in one place.
